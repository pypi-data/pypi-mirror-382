Security Tip

Your recovery codes serve as backup access in case you lose your two-factor authentication (2FA) device or app.

Store your recovery codes securely and do not share them with anyone. Treat them like sensitive passwords.

If you believe your codes have been exposed or lost, generate new recovery codes or remove or invalidate those codes immediately.

Be cautious of phishing attempts any legitimate services will never ask you to provide your recovery codes.

Warning

Keeping your recovery codes safe ensures uninterrupted access to your account under all circumstances.
